## Paper metadata (synthetic)

**Title:** Long-term effect of nintedanib on FVC decline in patients with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis: a 5-year follow-up of the INPULSIS-ON cohort
**Authors:** López-Hernández et al.
**Journal:** European Respiratory Journal, 2024
**Study type:** Prospective open-label extension cohort
**Population:** 1 057 patients with IPF previously enrolled in INPULSIS trials
**Intervention:** Nintedanib 150 mg twice daily, long-term
**Comparator:** Historical pre-treatment slope (within-patient)
**Primary outcome:** Annual rate of FVC decline (mL/year)
**Results:** Sustained reduction in FVC decline (-95 mL/year on treatment vs -223 mL/year pre-treatment, p<0.001); safety profile consistent with prior reports
